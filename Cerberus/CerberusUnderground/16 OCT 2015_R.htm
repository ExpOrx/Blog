Romanian actor p0s3id0n (aka viscolul), the developer of Cerebrus POS malware, linked to dump shop, numerous malicious domains and malware in the wild; likely linked to MWZLesson POS malware

16 Oct 2015 14:58:00 UTC
{ "tab": "raw" } Raw text { "tab": "comments" } Researcher comments

Previous reporting (see similar reports section) detailed actor p0s3id0n looking for partners to help spread a private POS malware the actor calls Cerebrus. p0s3id0n had provided a live demo of the Cerebrus panel that had exposed the fact it was being hosted at p0s3id0n.su. Further research into this domain identified a number of related malware samples, IP addresses and domains:

 

Through analysis of current and passive DNS records we identified the following domains we believe to be closely related:

p0s3id0n.su - actor p0s3id0n provided a demo of his private POS malare (Cerebrus) that exposed this domain. See previous reporting.
p0s3id0n.ru
forum1.p0s3id0n.su - numerous malware samples (see below) were observed as contacting this host.
psdumps.info - actor's dump shop called "Vendetta World".

Previous reporting detailed the working relationship between the actors 1nsider and p0s3id0n. p0s3id0n was previously soliciting partners to spread his private POS trojan (Cerebrus) while 1nsider was reportedly involved in running the dump shop. The shop is called Vendetta World and can be found at psdumps.info. Analysis of passive DNS records confirms a link between the domains associated with the dumpshop (psdumps.xx) and the domain (p0s3id0n.su) used to host the panel seen in a live demo by p0s3id0n. It's likely the actor's are using their dumpshop to monetize credit data stolen through the use of their POS malware.

 

Through analysis of Passive DNS we identified the following domains we believed are linked to trapjob101.com, which we believe is likely part of p0s3id0n's infrastructure. We are still trying to determine if they are definitively linked to the group, but it is worth noting that one of the domains (smboy.su) was publicly linked to the POS Malware dubbed MWZLesson by some vendors. See the following:

- https://twitter.com/snxperxero/statuses/635802404844359680
- https://www.virustotal.com/en/file/891F5C4CA2A835E32B773B6179B21E9737000A862BCE81229FBC8B8930992E38/analysis/

We are actively trying to obtain samples of the POS malware from p0s3id0n to determine for sure the actor is linked to the MWZLesson POS malware and if his Cerberus POS malware is what the AV/Research community refers to as MWZlesson. Further reporting will be forthcoming as more analysis is conducted on all of the date we have related to these actors and malware.  Maltego files are available upon request if needed.

 

** Note: the previous IR had indicated that the actor was Spanish; however, after further research we determined that the actor was Romanian and residing in Spain. The previous IR has been changed to reflect this new information. **
