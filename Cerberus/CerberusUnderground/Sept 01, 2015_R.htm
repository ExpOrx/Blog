Actor silthx registered on Alphabay on May 10, 2015 and holds a vendor tag in the community. He is advertising his new "SkyWyder Private RAT", which he claims is able to bypass UAC or BSOD. silthx also commented that this RAT is coded with custom functions in VB6. SkyWyder RAT is also designed to handle up to 20k bots by port. Upon generating a payload, actor claims his RAT can generate a payload as small as of 104kb. RAT is also able to steal bitcoin payments by replacing the bitcoin wallet address copied on a victim's clipboard to the attacker's wallet, so this RAT is capable of driving enough attention of many cyber criminals.


According to a reliable and sensitive source with direct access to the actor, silthx has gathered a team of professional coders from different places that are working together on the development of this RAT and other malware. Our source informed us that the actor claims this team has developed the SkyWyder RAT over the last 5 months. silthx has also been involved in activity related to locker malware and exploits in the past, but this is his current project.

 

silthx had sold 1 copy of SkyWyder RAT for $300 on Alphabay's Marketplace at the time of writing. He had received positive feedback for this sale. A list of actor's showing interest in buying SkyWyder RAT includes:

- BK201

Other interesting activity by silthx on Alphabay includes:

24 Aug 2015 - Selling custom phishing pages which can be made on request for any website for $200

 

On a question posted by actor JackPlume suggesting a crypter for this RAT, actor replied as:

---
We have our own crypter too, it's will be finish in the next days & we will sell it too also. Our team is new but each coder has great knowledge on hacking, so we can provide the best possible quality.
---
